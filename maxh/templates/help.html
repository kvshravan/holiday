Samay - Help

Samay helps you get maximum consecutive holidays with affordable leaves.

To start using the tool enter the year and the number of days of leave you are planning to use for the vacation/break (Max. affordable field)

To customise holidays/country go to View/Edit Holidays page. Here you can remove holidays, add any custom holiday and even add mutiple weekday holidays for an entire year.

By default All saturdays and sundays are considered holidays but for instance your company might be closed on every Monday and every Tuesday, you can remove All-sundays and All-saturdays from the list of holidays and add custom multiple holidays on "All-Mondays" and on "All-Tuesdays".
